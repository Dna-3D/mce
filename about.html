About the Department of Mechatronics Engineering

The Department of Mechatronics Engineering combines mechanical, electronic and software engineering to teach and develop modern automated systems. We prepare students for robotics, control systems, embedded design and industrial automation through hands-on labs, projects and industry collaboration.
